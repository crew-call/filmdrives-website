The Film Drives '2TB NVME SSD' is a portable, on-set solid state drive that delivers consistent offload speeds. Utilising under clocked NVMe Storage alongside a superior thermal design allows for all day backups without compromise.

The Film Drives '2TB NVME SSD' boasts 960MB/s Write Speeds and 950MB/s Read Speeds. For maximum performance it comes equipped with a USB-C Port to leverage the faster speeds offered by USB 3.2 Gen 2. Compatible with Thunderbolt 3/4 Ports as well as older USB-A Ports (via a USB-C to USB-A Cable).
